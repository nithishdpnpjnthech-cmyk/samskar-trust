About Samsakar Trust

0
Years of Service

Making a Difference, One Life at a Time

Samsakar Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through compassion and action. Founded with a vision to create lasting change, we work tirelessly to provide essential support to those who need it most.

Our mission encompasses medical aid, food support, education, and women empowerment. Every contribution, every volunteer hour, and every act of kindness brings us closer to a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."

- Mahatma Gandhi
